Kate's Playlists

Hi! My name is Kate Schultz and this is my playlist portfolio. Since March of 2015 I've been using Spotify to chronicle my life in music, making a monthly playlist that captures a moment in time. When I decided to apply to this position I knew I wanted to share these playlists with you as an example of my passion for a wide variety of musical styles, talent for curation, and familiarity with the platform. While initially unsure of how to share this with you I decided to put my problem solving skills to use and challenge myself to code a website, something I have never done before. I hope this portfolio stands as an example of my passion, skill, ingenuity and creativity that I will bring to this team.
